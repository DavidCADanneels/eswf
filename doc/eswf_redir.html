Function Details

action/2

action(Url, X2::{Width, Height}) -> {do_action, Actions}

Return a do_action tag that does a loadMovie to Url with a registration point at the center. The center of the loaded movie clip will be at (0, 0).

swf/2

Equivalent to swf(Url, {Width, Height}, 12, 6).

swf/4

swf(Url, Dimensions::{Width, Height}, Fps, FlashVersion) -> iodata()

Return a SWF that does a loadMovie to Url with a registration point at the center. The center of the loaded movie clip will be at (0, 0).
