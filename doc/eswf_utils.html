edit/2

edit(Binary::binary(), Edits) -> iodata()

  • Edits = [{Begin::int(), End::int(), NewData::iodata()}]

Performs a series of subsequence substitutions on a binary value. For each tuple {Begin, End, NewData} in Edits, the byte range [Begin, End) in Binary is replaced by NewData.

The Begin and End values are always interpreted as offsets into the original binary value. Byte ranges in Edits must not overlap and must occur in monotonically increasing order.
